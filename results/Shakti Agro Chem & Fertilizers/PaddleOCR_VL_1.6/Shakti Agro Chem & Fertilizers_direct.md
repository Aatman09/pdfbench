Alert Notice No. 10/2024-25

Sub: Alert Notice regarding availment/passing on of Input Tax Credit (ITC) on the strength of bogus invoices without any supply of corresponding goods to/from M/s Shakti Agro Chem & Fertilizers (GSTIN:24DKMPP4401F1ZB) -reg.

Intelligence/information was received from the DGGI, Zonal Unit, Ahmedabad that various suppliers/aggregators are involved in the diversion of Agriculture Grade Urea (AGU) for industrial use, leading to loss to Government exchequer. Further, it was informed that commodity viz. Technical Grade Urea (TGU) falls under HSN 3102 and attracts 18% GST, whereas Agriculture Grade Urea also fall under HSN 3102 and the same is taxable @5% in GST. Intelligence further revealed that these firms were also involved in bogus invoice transactions to claim fake Input Tax Credit.

2. An investigation is carried out against one such firm in the name of M/s. MH Acid and Chemicals, Rajkot which is found to be involved in the availment of fake ITC from the fake/non-existent firms and diversion of AGU to TGU. Also, it was observed that M/s. MH Acid and Chemicals, Rajkot had shown inward supplies of TGU from M/s. Shakti Agro Chem and Fertilizers (GSTIN: 24DKMPP4401F1ZB). Accordingly, an investigation was initiated against the above said supplier firm viz. M/s. Shakti Agro Chem and Fertilizers. The said firm was registered as proprietary firm of Smt. Ilaben Patel and had obtained the GST registration on 13.09.2017. The registration was cancelled suo-moto effective from 01.10.2018.
